Frequently Asked Questions

The camera preview is black or frozen. What can I do?

Make sure MirrorBooth has camera permission in your device settings (Settings → Privacy → Camera), then restart the app.

Where are my photos and videos saved?

Captures are saved to your device's photo library when you tap save. MirrorBooth has no cloud storage — everything stays on your device.

Does MirrorBooth require an internet connection?

No. The mirror preview, effects, and capturing all work fully offline. Only the experimental video-call feature uses the network.

Which platforms are supported?

iOS and Android, with desktop builds for macOS, Windows, and Linux available at olin.now.

Privacy

MirrorBooth collects no personal data and processes all video on your device. See the Privacy Policy for details.
