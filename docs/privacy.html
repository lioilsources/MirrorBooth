Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 23, 2026

Overview

MirrorBooth is a camera app that shows a real-time symmetric mirror of your face. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. All image processing happens on your device.

Camera

MirrorBooth uses your device's camera to render the live mirror preview. The camera feed is processed entirely on your device and is never uploaded, analyzed remotely, or shared with anyone.

Photos and Videos

Photos and video clips you capture are saved to your device's photo library only when you choose to save them. They stay on your device — MirrorBooth has no servers and never uploads your media.

Microphone

Microphone access is requested only for recording video clips with sound and for the experimental video-call feature. Audio is never recorded or transmitted without your explicit action.

Video Calls

If you use the video-call feature, your camera and microphone streams are transmitted directly to the other participant using peer-to-peer WebRTC. A signaling server is used only to establish the connection; calls are not recorded, stored, or monitored.

Data We Do Not Collect

MirrorBooth does not collect:

— Personal identifiers (name, email, phone number)

— Location data

— Device identifiers

— Usage analytics or telemetry

Third-Party Services

MirrorBooth does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

Children

MirrorBooth does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. As no data is collected at all, the app is safe for all ages.

Changes to This Policy
